Design

Our design choices reflect our brand identity and values. We use a simple and elegant color scheme and typography that convey professionalism, trustworthiness, and innovation. We also use responsive design techniques that ensure our website looks great on any device or screen size.

Color Scheme

  • Dark blue: A stable, reliable, loyal, and confident color that reflects our communication and technology focus. We use it for our logo, headings, links, and buttons.
  • White: A clean, simple, clear, and pure color that reflects our space and light theme. We use to create contrast and readability.
  • Dark gray: A neutral, balanced, sophisticated, and professional color that reflects our modernity and elegance. We use it for our main content background to create a dark theme.
  • Yellow: A warm, energetic, optimistic, and happy color that reflects our attention and creativity. We use it as our accent color for our logo and some highlighted or emphasized elements.

Typography

  • Arial: A sans-serif font that is widely used on the web. It is easy to read, versatile, and modern. We use Arial as our default font for our website content.
  • Georgia: A serif font that is elegant, classic, and refined. It is suitable for headings, titles, and logos. We use Georgia as our alternative font for some elements that we want to stand out or add some style.
